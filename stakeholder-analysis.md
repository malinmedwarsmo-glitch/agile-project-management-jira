# 👥 Stakeholder Analysis – IT Transformation Project

## 1. Stakeholder Overview

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest | Influence | Category |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Project Manager | Leads the project | Very High | Very High | Internal |
| IT Department | Implements changes | High | Medium | Internal |
| Management / Sponsor | Approves budget & direction | Medium | Very High | Internal |
| End Users | Use the new system | High | Low | Internal |
| External Consultant | Provides expertise | Medium | Medium | External |

---

---

## 3. Stakeholder Details

### 🔵 Project Manager
- **Interest:** Full project success, on-time delivery
- **Influence:** High – makes all key decisions
- **Engagement Strategy:** Daily self-review, sprint ceremonies
- **Communication:** Daily updates via Jira board

### 🟢 IT Department
- **Interest:** Clear tasks, realistic workload
- **Influence:** Medium – executes the work
- **Engagement Strategy:** Regular sprint planning and reviews
- **Communication:** Weekly via Jira + Confluence

### 🔴 Management / Sponsor
- **Interest:** Project outcomes, ROI, risk management
- **Influence:** Very High – can stop or redirect project
- **Engagement Strategy:** Regular status updates, risk reporting
- **Communication:** Bi-weekly status report

### 🟡 End Users
- **Interest:** Easy-to-use systems, minimal disruption
- **Influence:** Low – but critical for adoption
- **Engagement Strategy:** Keep informed of changes
- **Communication:** Monthly newsletter / update email

### ⚪ External Consultant
- **Interest:** Deliver agreed scope
- **Influence:** Medium – advises but doesn't decide
- **Engagement Strategy:** Involve in planning phases
- **Communication:** As needed via email

---

## 4. Communication Plan

| Stakeholder | What | How | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Project Manager | Full project status | Jira Dashboard | Daily |
| IT Department | Sprint tasks & updates | Jira + Confluence | Weekly |
| Management | Project status report | Email / Confluence | Bi-weekly |
| End Users | Change updates | Email | Monthly |
| External Consultant | Scope & deliverables | Email / Meeting | As needed |
